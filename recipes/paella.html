Paella with Chicken and Shrimps

Description

The Spanish-inspired paella is packed with juicy chicken, shrimp, and flavourful saffron-infused rice. It's perfect dish for sharing with family or friends.

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts (cut into pieces)
  • 200g shrimps (peeled and deveined)
  • 1 1/2 cups paella rice
  • 3 cups chicken broth
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 red pepper (sliced)
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 2 tsbp olive oil
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas
  • lemon wedges

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add chicken and cook until golden. Remove and set aside.
  2. In the same pan, saute onion, garlic, and red pepper until soft.
  3. Add tomato, smoked paprika, stirring well.
  4. Pour in the rice and mix until coated with flavours.
  5. Add chicken broth, season with salt and pepper, and bring to a simmer.
  6. Place chicken back into the pan and let cook for about 15 minutes.
  7. Add shrimp and peas, cooking for another 5 minutes until shrimp turn pink.
  8. Remove from heat and let it rest for 5 minutes. Serve with lemon wedges.

Shakshuka

Description

Shakshuka is a delicious Middle Eastern dish made with poached eggs in a spicy tomato sauce, perfect for breakfast or brunch.

Ingredients

  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 red pepper (chopped)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan and saute onion, garlic, and red pepper until soft.
  2. Add cumin, paprika, and chili flakes, stirring well.
  3. Pour in the diced tomatoes, season with salt and pepper , and let simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Make small wells in the sauce and crack eggs into them.
  5. Cover the pan and cook until eggs are set.
  6. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve with crusty bread.

Tantanmen Ramen

Description

Tantanmen is a Japanese ramen with a spicy, creamy sesame-based broth and ground meat topping.

Ingredients

  • 200g ramen noodles
  • 200g ground pork or chicken
  • 3 cups chicken broth
  • 1 tbsp miso paste
  • 2 tbsp soyt sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame paste or tahini
  • 1 tbsp chili oil
  • 1 garlic clove (minced)
  • 1 spn ginger
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 soft boiled egg
  • 1 green onion

Steps

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a pan and cook ground meat with garlic and ginger until browned.
  2. Add miso paste and osy sauce, stirring well.
  3. In a pot, heat chicken broth, then mix in sesame paste and chili oil.
  4. Cook ramen noodles according to package instructions.
  5. Divide noodles into bowls, pour over the broth, and top with cooked meat.
  6. Add a soft boiled egg and garnish with green onion.

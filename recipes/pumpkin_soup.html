Pumpkin Soup

Description

A creamy and comforting soup made with pumpik, coconut milk, and warming spices. Perfect for a cozy evening.

Ingredients

  • 500g pumpkin (peeled and chopped)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 3 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp ground ginger
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • pumpkin seeds

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a pot and saute onion and garlic until soft.
  2. Add pumpkin, cumin, and ginger, cooking for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Popur in vegetable broth and bring to a boil. Simmer for 20 minutes until pumpkin is tender.
  4. Blend the soup until smooth, then stir in coconut milk.
  5. Season with salt and pepper, then simmer for 5 more minutes.
  6. Serve hot, garnished with pumpkin seeds.
